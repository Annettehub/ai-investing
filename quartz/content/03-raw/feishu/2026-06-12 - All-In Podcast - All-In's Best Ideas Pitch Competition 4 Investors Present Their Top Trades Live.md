# Takeaways

* Data centers function as modern refineries where electricity serves as the raw input and intelligence, in the form of tokens or photons, acts as the refined output, necessitating a massive, mandatory build-out of all power generation sources to support AI.
  > 数据中心像现代炼油厂，电力作为原材料，智能以代币或光子的形式作为精炼产出，这需要对所有发电源进行大规模、强制性的扩建，以支持人工智能。
* Buying hard assets at a significant discount to their replacement cost provides a powerful margin of safety, offering the potential for exponential returns once the market recognizes the asset's future scarcity and necessity.
  > 以显著低于其更换成本的价格购买实物资产提供了一种强大的安全边际，一旦市场认识到资产未来的稀缺性和必要性，便有潜在的指数回报。
* Modern radiopharmaceuticals operate like autonomous, GPS-guided micro-drones, enabling precise molecular recognition and the targeted destruction of tumor cells with minimal collateral damage compared to traditional, indiscriminate chemotherapy.
  > 现代放射性药物像自主的 GPS 导航微型无人机一样工作，能够实现精准的分子识别，并以比传统的、不加选择的化疗更小的附带伤害，针对性地摧毁肿瘤细胞。
* Decentralized incentive models, powered by token-based rewards, allow for the rapid and cost-efficient deployment of global physical infrastructure networks that would otherwise be prohibitively expensive for traditional, centralized corporations to build.
  > 去中心化激励模型，由基于代币的奖励驱动，允许快速且具成本效益地部署全球物理基础设施网络，这些网络对于传统的集中式企业来说建造成本 prohibitive。
* Achieving "platform status" in biotech—where a company’s underlying technology can be applied to multiple, diverse targets—is the holy grail for valuation, as it allows investors to price in the promise of future breakthroughs rather than just current clinical results.
  > 在生物技术领域实现 “平台状态”——一个公司的基础技术可以应用于多个不同的目标——是估值的圣杯，因为这使投资者可以将未来突破的承诺放入价格中，而不仅仅是当前的临床结果。
* Certain critical technologies, such as specialized radioisotope payloads for cancer treatment, possess natural competitive moats because their manufacturing supply chains are localized to the U.S. and are extremely difficult for international competitors to replicate.
  > 某些关键技术，例如用于癌症治疗的专用放射性同位素有效载荷，拥有天然的竞争壁垒，因为它们的制造供应链仅限于美国，国际竞争者极难复制。
* Market valuation for major international infrastructure projects, such as new casino developments, typically begins to price in the asset's potential value approximately three years before the project becomes operational.
  > 重大国际基础设施项目的市场估值，例如新的赌场开发，通常在项目运营前大约三年时开始对资产的潜在价值进行定价。
* High-precision geolocation, specifically real-time kinematics (RTK), is becoming a foundational requirement for the next wave of robotics, including autonomous vehicles and agricultural drones, creating natural monopolies for the most ubiquitous and lowest-cost network providers.
  > 高精度地理定位，尤其是实时动态测量 (RTK)，正成为下一波机器人技术的基础要求，包括自主车辆和农业无人机，为最普遍和最低成本的网络提供商创造自然垄断。

# Q & A

**Q: Why is the massive increase in AI demand creating such a significant opportunity in the power sector, specifically for companies like Talon Energy?**
> **Q: 为什么 AI 需求的大幅增加在电力行业创造了如此重要的机会，特别是对于像 Talon Energy 这样的公司？**

A: We are entering a new technological power cycle where demand is set to explode. AI doesn't just increase demand; it "turbocharges" it, creating chronic shortages. Talon Energy is uniquely positioned because they own two gigawatts of nuclear power and six gigawatts of natural gas baseload power—hard assets that are currently trading at a significant discount to their replacement cost. My thesis is that if you buy these assets at a discount and hold them while the market builds new capacity, you can eventually sell them at a massive premium. Even if the company does nothing but run its current business, it generates substantial free cash flow, but the real upside comes from signing premium contracts with data centers that are currently in a panic to secure power.
> A: 我们正进入一个新的技术电力周期，需求将会激增。AI 不仅增加需求；它 “加速” 了需求，造成了持续的短缺。Talon Energy 独具优势，因为他们拥有两吉瓦的核电和六吉瓦的天然气基荷电力——这些硬资产目前的交易价格显著低于其更换成本。我的论点是，如果你以折扣价购买这些资产，并在市场建立新产能的同时持有它们，最终可以以巨额溢价出售。即使公司什么也不做，仅仅运营现有业务，也能产生可观的自由现金流，但真正的上升空间来自与数据中心签署高价合同，它们目前正处于为了获得电力而慌乱不已的状态。

**Q: What makes MGM Resorts a compelling investment beyond its traditional casino business in Las Vegas?**
> **Q: 除了在拉斯维加斯的传统赌场业务外，是什么让 MGM Resorts 成为一个引人注目的投资？**

A: Most people view MGM solely as a Vegas play, but the real value lies in two hidden assets: their license to open a casino in Osaka, Japan, and the potential for a future casino in Dubai. Japan is a massive, first-world gambling market—pachinko and horse racing alone account for a $40 billion market—and Osaka is geographically closer to major Chinese gambling hubs than Macau or Singapore. Furthermore, Barry Diller’s aggressive acquisition of MGM stock acts as a catalyst and provides a floor for the valuation. We believe the stock is currently mispriced because the market is not yet fully accounting for the massive EBITDA potential of the Japanese opportunity, which could effectively triple the value of the company over time.
> A: 大多数人只把 MGM 视为拉斯维加斯的投资，但真正的价值在于两个隐藏的资产：他们在日本大阪开设赌场的许可证，以及未来在迪拜赌场的潜力。日本是一个巨大的第一世界赌博市场，仅仅是弹珠机和赛马就占据了 400 亿美元的市场，而大阪在地理上更靠近主要的中国赌博中心，比澳门或新加坡更近。此外，Barry Diller 对 MGM 股票的激进收购作为催化剂，为估值提供了支撑。我们认为，股票目前被低估，因为市场尚未完全考虑到日本机会的庞大 EBITDA 潜力，这可能会在未来有效提高公司的价值三倍。

**Q: How does GeoNet use a decentralized crypto model to build a high-precision geolocation network that outperforms traditional GPS?**
> **Q: GeoNet 如何利用去中心化的加密模型建立高精度的地理定位网络，从而超越传统 GPS？**

A: Standard GPS has a precision of about two meters, which is insufficient for modern robotics. GeoNet utilizes Real-Time Kinematics (RTK) to achieve accuracy down to two centimeters. We built the world’s largest and fastest-growing RTK network not by acting like a traditional telecom company, but by incentivizing individuals and small businesses to host base stations on their properties in exchange for GeoTokens. This decentralized model gives us the lowest cost structure in the industry. We are essentially building a natural monopoly; we’ve already covered 80% of the global population, and our revenue is growing 3x year-over-year, with 80% of that revenue being used to buy back tokens from the open market, creating a direct value accrual mechanism for token holders.
> A: 标准 GPS 的精度约为两米，这对于现代机器人技术来说不足够。GeoNet 利用实时动态定位（RTK）实现精度达到两厘米。我们建立了世界上最大、增长最快的 RTK 网络，不是通过传统电信公司的方式，而是通过激励个人和小企业在其物业上托管基站以换取 GeoTokens。这种去中心化的模型使我们在行业中拥有最低的成本结构。我们本质上是在创建一个自然垄断；我们已经覆盖了全球 80% 的人口，收入同比增长三倍，其中 80% 的收入用于从公开市场回购代币，为代币持有者创造直接的价值累积机制。

**Q: What sets Actis Oncology apart in the notoriously difficult biotech sector, and why is their approach to radiotherapy considered a "moat"?**
> **Q: 在生物技术行业 notoriously 难以进入的情况下，是什么让 Actis Oncology 与众不同，他们在放射治疗方面的做法又为何被视为 “护城河”？**

A: Biotech is often like a casino, but we focus on margin of safety. Actis Oncology is developing a platform of radio-pharmaceuticals—essentially "micro-drones" that navigate the bloodstream to precisely target and detonate cancer cells with minimal collateral damage. The "moat" here is twofold: first, the platform is designed to be highly specific, allowing physicians to verify target engagement in real-time through imaging, which significantly de-risks clinical trials. Second, the radioisotopes used, specifically actinium, are incredibly difficult to replicate and are effectively off-limits to competitors in China because the supply chain is tied to legacy US nuclear programs. This creates a barrier to entry that is rare in the biotech world.
> A: 生物技术行业常常像赌场，但我们关注的是安全边际。Actis Oncology 正在开发一种放射性药物平台——本质上是 “微型无人机”，它们在血液中导航，精确瞄准并引爆癌细胞，造成最小的附带损伤。“护城河” 有两个方面：首先，该平台被设计为高度特异性，使医生能够通过影像实时验证目标的参与，从而显著降低临床试验的风险。其次，使用的放射性同位素，特别是铀，是非常难以复制的，而且由于供应链依赖于历史美国核计划，它们基本上对中国的竞争者封闭。这在生物技术世界中创造了一个罕见的进入壁垒。

**Q: How do you evaluate the regulatory risks associated with the massive power demands of AI data centers?**
> **Q: 你如何评估与 AI 数据中心的巨大电力需求相关的监管风险？**

A: Regulatory risk is real, but human ingenuity usually finds a way. The current bottleneck is that power prices are still too low to stimulate the massive new capacity required. The working solution for data centers is to sign long-term Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs) while incorporating battery storage and peaker plants to handle peak demand hours. While consumer power bills might rise slightly, they will remain under control. We need AI for national security and scientific breakthroughs, so the build-out is mandatory. The market is essentially forcing a deal between power producers and hyperscalers, and companies like Talon are in the pole position to capture that value.
> A: 监管风险是真实存在的，但人类的创造力通常会找到解决办法。目前的瓶颈是，电价仍然太低，无法刺激所需的大规模新产能。数据中心的有效解决方案是签署长期电力购置协议（PPA），同时结合电池存储和峰时发电厂来处理峰值需求时段。尽管消费者的电费可能会略微上涨，但仍会保持在可控范围内。我们需要 AI 来保障国家安全和科学突破，因此这一建设是必须的。市场实质上是在强迫电力生产商与超大规模公司达成交易，而像 Talon 这样的公司正处于捕获这一价值的有利位置。

**Q: Why is the "replacement cost" strategy a reliable playbook for long-term investing?**
> **Q: 为什么 “更换成本” 策略是长期投资可靠的操作手册？**

A: It’s a strategy I learned from Sam Zell: if you can buy a hard asset at a significant discount to what it would cost to build it today, and that asset is essential for the future, you have a winning hand. You buy at a discount, hold it, and eventually sell it at a big premium to replacement cost when the market finally wakes up to the scarcity of that capacity. We applied this to real estate in the past, and we are applying it now to power generation. When you see an enterprise value of $25 billion for assets that would cost $45 billion to build, the math is simple, and the risk-reward profile is incredibly favorable.
> A: 这是我从 Sam Zell 那里学到的策略：如果你能以显著低于今天建造成本的价格购买一项硬资产，并且该资产对未来至关重要，那么你就拥有了一手好牌。你以折扣价购买，持有它，最终以高于更换成本的溢价出售，当市场最终意识到这种产能的稀缺性时。我们过去将这种策略应用于房地产，现在我们将其应用于发电。当你看到企业价值为 250 亿美元，而构建这些资产的成本为 450 亿美元时，数学是简单的，风险回报曲线极其有利。

**Q: How do you address the concern that satellite-based mesh networks might eventually replace ground-based RTK networks like GeoNet?**
> **Q: 你如何应对卫星网络最终可能取代像 GeoNet 这样的地面 RTK 网络的担忧？**

A: There is simply no way for satellite-based systems to compete on cost. GeoNet base stations cost a few hundred dollars to deploy, whereas launching satellites into space is an incredibly capital-intensive endeavor. Furthermore, energy consumption is a major factor; for battery-sensitive applications like drones or autonomous vehicles, a ground-based solution will always be more energy-efficient than relying on a signal from space. While satellites are great for ubiquity, they cannot match the cost-efficiency and precision of a dense, ground-based RTK network for the foreseeable future.
> A: 卫星系统根本无法在成本上进行竞争。GeoNet 基站的部署成本只需几百美元，而将卫星发射到太空是一项极其资本密集的工作。此外，能耗是一个重要因素；对于像无人机或自动驾驶汽车这样的对电池敏感的应用，基于地面的解决方案总是比依赖来自太空的信号更加节能。虽然卫星在普及度上表现良好，但在可预见的未来，它们无法匹配密集的地面 RTK 网络的成本效益和精度。

**Q: In the context of biotech investing, how do you handle the "China risk" where patents are often replicated?**
> **Q: 在生物技术投资的背景下，你如何处理 “中国产权风险”，因其专利常被复制？**

A: The China risk is a legitimate concern in many areas of biotech, especially with biologics where small changes to an amino acid can bypass composition-of-matter patents. However, with radio-pharmaceuticals, the reliance on specific, locally sourced radioisotopes acts as a natural safeguard. Because these isotopes are not readily available or easily manufactured in other regions, it is much harder for generic competitors to enter the market. This is why we view the Actis platform as having a more durable competitive advantage compared to traditional biotech companies that are constantly battling replication.
> A: 中国风险在生物技术的许多领域都是一个合理的担忧，尤其是生物制剂方面，因为对氨基酸的小改变可以绕过物质组成专利。然而，在放射性药物方面，对特定本地来源的放射性同位素的依赖充当了天然的保护措施。因为这些同位素在其他地区并不容易获得或制造，通用竞争者进入市场的难度大大加大。这就是为什么我们认为 Actis 平台相比传统生物技术公司更具持久的竞争优势，后者不断与复制进行斗争。
