## Startup Sustainability and the Impact of Capital Abundance


创业可持续性与资本丰盈的影响

Recruiting top-tier AI and machine learning talent has become increasingly difficult due to aggressive compensation packages from both model labs and well-funded startups. The current overabundance of capital in the startup ecosystem creates unsustainable structures, as companies pay premium salaries that do not align with long-term business viability. While high valuations can serve as a signal to prospective employees, they also create pressure to scale rapidly. The future of work will likely favor composite roles that generalize across traditional specializations, such as combining engineering, product management, and design, leading to more efficient, albeit smaller, teams.


由于模型实验室和资金充足的创业公司提供了激进的薪酬方案，招聘顶尖的人工智能和机器学习人才变得愈加困难。当前创业生态系统中资本的过剩创造了不可持续的结构，因为公司支付的高薪与长期商业生存能力并不一致。虽然高估值可以作为对潜在员工的信号，但它们也造成快速扩张的压力。未来的工作可能会更倾向于跨传统专业化的复合角色，例如结合工程、产品管理和设计，从而导致更高效但规模更小的团队。
