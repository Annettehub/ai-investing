# Q & A


Q: How do you achieve the most significant performance gains in AI infrastructure?


Q: 如何在人工智能基础设施中实现最显著的性能提升？

A: The real breakthrough innovation happens through software-hardware co-design. You cannot achieve massive gains by just improving one layer in isolation. If you get a 2x improvement in the model, a 2x in software, and a 2x in hardware, you end up with an 8x improvement. But when you co-optimize all three layers simultaneously—aligning the model architecture with the specific silicon and infrastructure software—you can achieve a 100x gain. The best labs are those that treat the model, the software, and the hardware as a single, unified design problem.
