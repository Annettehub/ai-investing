突发快讯：英伟达大规模铺设暗光纤项目属实

此前有同业机构披露，英伟达正在推进一套自研暗光纤网络搭建项目，大量投资者致电我方求证该消息真实性。通过对接行业产业链人脉，我方现已确认该项目真实落地。

英伟达自研长距暗光纤网络项目确已落地。根据行业渠道信息，英伟达正在全美批量采购长途暗光纤，单批次光纤规格最高可达100芯光纤对。

目前产业链各方暂未完全明确英伟达大规模囤货暗光纤的核心目的，我方判断其核心诉求是打通GPU算力生态全链路的网络瓶颈。英伟达此前已与Lumentum、相干Coherent、康宁Corning达成多项光通信合作，也侧面印证其对高速光互联基础设施的布局决心。

我们推测，本次光纤基建核心服务于英伟达NeoCloud新云业务客户：与亚马逊、微软等传统超大规模云厂商不同，NeoCloud合作企业自身储备的光纤网络带宽资源严重不足，英伟达需要自建链路补足客户算力互联能力。

同时不排除其他战略动机，其中市场认可度最高的另一套逻辑是：英伟达意在自建网络基础设施，切入GPU即服务（GPU-as-a-Service）公有云赛道，直接与传统云厂商竞争。

Wolfe Byte: A competitor recently pointed to a potential dark fiber network buildout being undertaken by NVIDIA. We picked up a number of inbound calls from investors questioning the veracity of that call. Based on our conversations with industry contacts, we can confirm this project is real. Our favorite play on this NVIDIA deployment is Ciena.

The NVIDIA Dark Fiber Buildout is Real... Based on our conversations, we understand that NVIDIA is buying up long haul dark fiber all over the United States – with fiber counts ranging up to 100 pairs. Our industry contacts do not know why NVIDIA is buying up dark fiber. Our sense is that NVIDIA (NVDA, covered by Chris Caso) is trying to resolve bottlenecks across their GPU ecosystem. The GPU suppliers' recent deals with Lumentum, Coherent, and Corning offer other recent examples. We presume this effort is oriented around NVIDIA's NeoCloud customers which – unlike the Hyperscalers – have been slower to lock up dark fiber network capacity themselves. We recognize there are other potential reasons why NVIDIA could be doing this. Competing in the GPU-as-a-Service market has been raised as a potential alternative explanation, for example.
